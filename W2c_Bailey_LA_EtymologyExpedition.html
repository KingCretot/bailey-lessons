🏗V · Sentence Architect

Strong writers combine short choppy sentences into one smooth sentence. Joining words like and, but, because, so, when, although connect ideas. A compound sentence joins two complete ideas; a complex sentence adds a dependent idea.
📖 COMBINING EXAMPLE
Choppy: "The fox was hungry. The hens were safe."
Combined (compound): "The fox was hungry, but the hens were safe."
Combined (complex): "Although the fox was hungry, the hens were safe."
