🧮 Money Math

Money uses decimals: $3.50 means 3 dollars and 50 cents. To find change, subtract what you spent from what you had.

✍️ Real World

Writing the Trip Report

Reports must be clear and correct. That means conjunctions, verb tense, and subject-verb agreement. 📄

🔗 Joining Words (FANBOYS)

Coordinating conjunctions join ideas: for, and, nor, but, or, yet, so. Example: "I wanted to hike, but it rained."

Decoding the Animal Facts

Real readers pull main ideas and conclusions out of nonfiction. Let's decode some animal facts! 📚

📚 Read this:

The aardvark sleeps in its burrow all day. At night, it comes out to dig for ants and termites with its strong claws.

Main idea: the aardvark is active at night. Key details: sleeps by day, hunts at night.
