🔢 Decimals

Decimals to Hundredths

A decimal shows part of a whole using place value. The first place after the dot is tenths; the second is hundredths. 🧮

🧮 Reading 3.47

3ones — left of the dot
.44 tenths
.077 hundredths
Compareline up the dots, compare left to right

Tip: 0.5 and 0.50 are equal — extra zeros on the right don't change the value.

🔄 Fraction–Decimal

The Fraction–Decimal Connection

Fractions with denominators of 10 or 100 turn straight into decimals. 7/10 = 0.7 and 23/100 = 0.23. Same amount, two outfits! 🎭

🔄 Matching Pairs

3/4 = 75/100 = 0.75three fourths
9/100 = 0.09nine hundredths

📐 Area & Perimeter

Measuring Shapes

Perimeter is the distance AROUND a shape — add all sides. Area is the space INSIDE — for a rectangle, multiply length × width. 📐

🟩 A Safari Enclosure: 8 m by 5 m

Perimeter8 + 5 + 8 + 5 = 26 m of fence
Area8 × 5 = 40 square meters of ground
Perimeter unitsm, ft, cm — a length
Area unitssquare m, square ft — a space

🐘 Try it!

📐 Geometry

Angles & Lines

Geometry describes the shapes of the wild. Angles can be right, acute, or obtuse, and lines can be parallel or perpendicular. 📐

📐 Know Your Angles

Right angleexactly 90° — a perfect corner
Acute anglesmaller than 90° — sharp
Obtuse anglebigger than 90° — wide open
Straight angleexactly 180° — a flat line

🛤️ Lines

Parallel lines never cross — like two safari trails side by side.
Perpendicular lines cross to make a right angle — like a plus sign.

🧠 Word Problems

Multi-Step Safari Problems

Put it all together: read carefully, plan your steps, and check that the answer makes sense for the question. 🗺️

🐘 Worked Example

A rectangular watering hole is 12 m by 8 m. A ranger walks all the way around it 3 times. How far does she walk?

Step 1: Perimeter = 12 + 8 + 12 + 8 = 40 m.

Step 2: 40 × 3 = 120 m.
