How points work ▶

Every lesson is a chance to earn Showdown Points. Both girls have the same points available every week — the Showdown is always fair.

  • Lesson Score — up to 100 pts from the interactive lesson (every correct answer, game, and challenge counts).
  • Post-Lesson Assignment — 50 pts for finishing the handwritten assignment and turning it in at the next session.
  • Handwriting Star — up to 25 pts for the neatest, most careful handwriting (letters AND numbers).
  • Reading Bonus — +10 pts each time a book is read and told to Mr. King-Cretot.

Each lesson is worth up to 175 pts; each week (Math + LA) up to 350 pts, plus reading bonuses. The girl with the higher weekly total earns the Weekly Star ⭐.

Championship — Week 9 (Aug 10): every point is DOUBLED (up to 700 on the line). The champion earns the trophy ceremony, a certificate, and a full year of bragging rights.
